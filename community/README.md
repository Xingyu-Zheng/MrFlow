# Community Contributions

This space collects community work that brings MrFlow to more model families, runtimes, and workflow systems.

We welcome community implementations, ComfyUI workflows, and reproduction experiments for new models and runtime environments. The goal is to make useful community work visible early, and to give promising ideas a place to grow before selected contributions are tested and polished.

- `experimental/`: early community contributions, prototypes, and workflows that are useful to share before broader testing.
- `verified/`: community contributions that have been tried by the maintainers and are ready for broader reuse.

Selected pieces may also be promoted into the main `examples/` or `ComfyUI-MrFlow/` folders when they become part of the official workflow.

Pull requests are the easiest way to contribute. If you are not familiar with PRs, opening an issue with a link to your code or workflow and tagging the maintainers is also welcome.

## Contributions

We thank all contributors who share implementations, workflows, experiments, or issue-linked prototypes with the project, whether or not a contribution has already moved into `verified/` or the main repository.

- [Apolinario](https://github.com/apolinario) from the Hugging Face open-source team: built an interactive MrFlow Space on Hugging Face Spaces and opened the transfer discussion in [issue #1](https://github.com/Xingyu-Zheng/MrFlow/issues/1).
- [RealRebelAI](https://github.com/RealRebelAI): contributed [Rebels_MrFlow](https://github.com/RealRebelAI/Rebels_MrFlow), a community ComfyUI extension and workflows exploring MrFlow-style staged sampling for Krea-2 and Z-Image Turbo.
